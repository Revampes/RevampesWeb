What is AfterTimeFault?

AfterTimeFault is a modern Hypixel Skyblock client mod focused on practical quality-of-life upgrades, smart automation helpers, high-clarity rendering tweaks, and performance-focused adjustments. Whether you grind Dungeons, Kuudra, Fishing, or general Skyblock progression, the mod bundles modular tools to streamline repetitive interactions while keeping control in the player's hands.

  • Category-based modular feature system (enable only what you need)
  • Smart automation helpers (multiple actions still under your intent)
  • Combat, utility, rendering & performance modules
  • Clear visual overlays & custom UI components
  • Designed to remain lean and responsive
